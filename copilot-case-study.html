The Challenge

Manual Chart Review Bottleneck

ProvidenceCare System's Expert Clinical Review nurses were overwhelmed by the time-intensive process of manually reviewing triggered charts. Each verification took nearly 30 minutes, with inconsistent documentation quality across reviewers.

28 min Average per chart
3.2 Document revisions
2,450 Historical verified events

Core Problems:

  • Manual Signal Hunting: Nurses spent excessive time searching through lengthy charts for relevant evidence
  • Inconsistent Documentation: Variable narrative tone and format across different reviewers
  • Repetitive Data Entry: Constant re-entry of demographic and basic patient information
  • Low Throughput: Limited number of charts processed per shift due to time constraints
