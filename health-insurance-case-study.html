Transforming Blue Cross Blue Shield of North Carolina's online insurance shopping experience to reduce customer support burden and increase self-service enrollments.

The Challenge

Manual Insurance Shopping Bottleneck

Blue Cross Blue Shield of North Carolina's customers struggled to compare insurance plans and complete applications online through BCBSNC.com. The majority of potential customers were forced to call sales representatives to purchase insurance, creating bottlenecks and increasing operational costs.

85% Phone-based sales
3.2 Average support calls
45 min Average call time

Core Problems:

  • Complex Plan Comparison: Customers couldn't effectively compare plan options and understand coverage differences
  • Insurance Terminology: Complex insurance terms created confusion and required support calls
  • No Decision Support: Lack of clear guidance for different customer scenarios
  • Cost Estimation: Inadequate tools for understanding total out-of-pocket costs
