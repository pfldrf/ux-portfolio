BCBSNC.com Insurance Shopping Platform

Transforming Blue Cross Blue Shield of North Carolina's online insurance shopping experience to reduce customer support burden and increase self-service enrollments.

Winner of the Interactive Media Awards Outstanding Achievement – Insurance Category

The Challenge

Manual Insurance Shopping Bottleneck

Blue Cross Blue Shield of North Carolina's customers struggled to compare insurance plans and complete applications online through BCBSNC.com. The majority of potential customers were forced to call sales representatives to purchase insurance, creating bottlenecks and increasing operational costs.

  • Complex Plan Comparison: Customers couldn't effectively compare plan options and understand coverage differences
  • Insurance Terminology: Complex insurance terms created confusion and required support calls
  • No Decision Support: Lack of clear guidance for different customer scenarios
  • Cost Estimation: Inadequate tools for understanding total out-of-pocket costs

Research & Insights

  • Support Call Analysis: Analyzed 500 support calls to identify common questions and points of confusion in the shopping process. 45 min average call time, 3.2 calls per enrollment
  • Usability Testing: Conducted 15 usability sessions to identify interface issues and opportunities for improvement. Plan comparison = 65% of shopping time
  • Competitive Analysis: Evaluated 8 competitor insurance sites to identify best practices and opportunities for differentiation. Focus on self-service + decision support

Design Process

1

Information Architecture

Re‑mapped the entire BCBSNC.com site to consolidate plan information into clear thematic groups and simplified global navigation, setting the foundation for a smoother shopping journey.

  • Logical plan grouping & URL hierarchy
  • Glossary / self‑service help structure
  • Quote‑to‑cart flow audit
2

Wireframe Sprint

Ran a one‑week design sprint to create mid‑fidelity wireframes for the end‑to‑end plan‑shopping experience, introducing split‑screen plan cards and interactive filters.

  • Rapid ideation workshops
  • Clickable Figma prototype
  • Remote stakeholder reviews
3

Decision Support

Built the interactive "Help Me Choose" wizard that compared deductibles, out‑of‑pocket max, and premium trade‑offs for users based on a short quiz about budget and expected care usage.

  • Dynamic cost calculator
  • Plan recommendation algorithm
  • A/B copy testing for clarity

Solution

Intuitive Plan Shopping Experience

The redesigned BCBSNC.com insurance shopping platform transformed the customer journey by providing clear plan comparisons, interactive cost tools, and streamlined enrollment—enabling customers to make informed decisions without requiring phone support. Much of the design we implemented in 2010, is still relevant today.
