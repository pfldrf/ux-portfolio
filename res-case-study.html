Adverse-Event Sentinel (AES)

Real-Time Risk Management Dashboard transforming how healthcare systems manage patient safety and risk by automatically detecting and validating adverse events for immediate intervention.

Client MetroHealth Alliance
Scale 18 hospitals, 2.3M encounters
Role Senior UX Researcher
Domain Risk Management

The Challenge

Reactive Risk Management

MetroHealth Alliance's risk management teams were trapped in a reactive cycle. Critical adverse events often went undetected for days, missing crucial intervention opportunities and increasing legal exposure.

11 days Average detection time
14% Events reviewed in-house
410 Monthly paper incident reports

Key Problems:

  • Delayed Response: Risk teams spent days manually reviewing incident reports
  • Missed Opportunities: Many preventable events weren't caught until after discharge
  • Resource Intensive: Manual review of hundreds of reports weekly
  • System Silos: Critical information spread across disconnected systems
"We were always fighting fires instead of preventing them. By the time we knew about an issue, the patient was already home." ‚Äî Risk Manager, MetroHealth Alliance

Real-Time Risk Intelligence

AES transforms reactive risk management into proactive patient safety by monitoring 42 structured data signals and 19 free-text analysis patterns in real-time, immediately alerting teams to high-risk situations.
