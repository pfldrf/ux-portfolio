Portfolio / Payment Integrity Compass

Payment Integrity Compass (PIC)

A revenue cycle management application that helps healthcare systems identify and recover millions in underpaid insurance claims through intelligent data analysis and streamlined workflows.

Client Healthcare Revenue Cycle SaaS
Alpha Partner Cleveland Clinic Foundation
Role UX Lead

The Challenge

Inefficient Underpayment Recovery

Large healthcare systems routinely experience significant underpayments from insurance providers, but lack efficient systems to identify and recover these lost revenues. Traditional claim identification and recovery processes are time-intensive and resource-heavy, making it impractical to pursue smaller-dollar underpayments despite their cumulative impact.

9 min Per claim review
68% Claims ignored
$28M Annual underpayments

Key Problems:

  • Resource Intensive: Manual claim review taking 9+ minutes per case
  • Missed Opportunities: 68% of underpayments ignored due to manual review costs
  • Lack of Visibility: No systematic way to track and prioritize recovery efforts
  • Inefficient Workflows: No bulk processing capabilities for similar underpayments

Discovery & Research

User Interviews

Conducted one-on-one interviews with healthcare executives, revenue cycle managers, and business office staff across multiple health systems including Cleveland Clinic Foundation.

Mental models for primary and secondary personas

Process Analysis

Mapped existing claim review workflows and identified why hospitals don't pursue "small-dollar" underpayments due to manual appeal costs.

68% of claims ignored due to manual review costs

Mental Model Development

Created mental model diagrams based on Indi Young's methodology to understand how revenue recovery specialists and executives think about maximizing contract value.

Tasks, goals, and beliefs mapped for primary users

Opportunity Analysis

Analyzed historical underpayment patterns to identify bulk appeal opportunities and define algorithms for the "opportunity engine".

$28M annual underpayments identified at alpha partner

Design Process

1

Mental Model Development

Created comprehensive mental model diagrams based on user interviews to understand how revenue recovery specialists and executives think about underpayment identification and collection processes.

  • User interview transcripts and recordings
  • Mental model diagrams for primary and secondary personas
  • Task, goal, and belief mapping
2

Process Flow Design

Designed innovative bulk appeal workflow that enables hospitals to collect on "small-dollar" underpayments by grouping similar claims into "Appeal Projects" for efficient processing.

  • Process flow diagrams
  • Bulk appeal workflow design
  • Opportunity engine algorithm definition
3

Information Architecture

Created comprehensive site maps and wireframes for the Variance Explorer dashboard and executive reporting interfaces, focusing on intuitive navigation and data visualization.

  • Site maps and information architecture
  • Wireframes and design specifications
  • Interactive dashboard prototypes
4

Data Visualization & Algorithm Refinement

Developed sophisticated data visualization concepts and refined opportunity engine algorithms through iterative testing with Cleveland Clinic Foundation, leading to immediate ROI.

  • Executive dashboard design with dark theme
  • Algorithm refinement for false positive reduction
  • Usability testing and optimization

The Solution

Intelligent Revenue Recovery Platform

Payment Integrity Compass transforms revenue cycle management from a reactive, manual process to a proactive, data-driven operation. The platform automatically identifies recoverable underpayments while eliminating claims with low recovery potential.

Opportunity Engine

  • Automated claim variance detection
  • Recovery probability scoring
  • Bulk processing capabilities
  • Workflow automation

Variance Explorer

  • Interactive data visualization
  • Advanced filtering and sorting
  • Line-item comparison views
  • Executive dashboards
